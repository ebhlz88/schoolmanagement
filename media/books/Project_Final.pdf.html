About Our University

We are one of the first engineering universities in the province with over 3,516 graduates serving in different organizations all over the globe.

Balochistan University of Engineering and Technology Khuzdar is a well-known public sector university providing a technical base and research platform to youngsters and contributing its due share in socio-economic uplift of Pakistan in general, and Balochistan in particular.
